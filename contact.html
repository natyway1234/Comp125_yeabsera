Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book classes in advance?

For most classes, advance booking is recommended to secure your spot. You can book through our mobile app or by calling the front desk.

What should I bring to my first visit?

Bring a water bottle, towel, and comfortable workout clothes. We provide all necessary equipment, but you're welcome to bring your own if preferred.

Is there a trial period?

Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial for new members. This gives you full access to our facilities and classes to see if we're the right fit.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, we offer membership freezes for medical reasons or extended travel. Contact our membership team for details on freeze policies.

Do you offer personal training?

Absolutely! We have certified personal trainers available for one-on-one sessions. Premium and Elite members receive complimentary sessions.

What safety measures are in place?

We maintain strict cleaning protocols, provide sanitization stations throughout the facility, and ensure all equipment is regularly sanitized.
